# Sub Text

Sub Text provides styling for subtext within HTML elements.

## Example

<span className="sub-text">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</span>
<br />
<br />

```jsx
<span class="sub-text">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</span>
```

## Sizes

<span className="sub-text sub-text-sm">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</span>
<br />
<span className="sub-text">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</span>
<br />
<span className="sub-text sub-text-lg">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</span>
<br />
<br />

```jsx
<span class="sub-text sub-text-sm">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</span>

<span class="sub-text">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</span>

<span class="sub-text sub-text-lg">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</span>
```

## Other Variants

<span className="sub-text sub-text-alt">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</span>
<br />
<br />

```jsx
<span class="sub-text sub-text-alt">
  The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
</span>
```
